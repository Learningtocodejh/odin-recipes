Pasta

Aglio Olio, an easy pasta to make for any occasion

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Salt
  • Minced garlic
  • Chili

Steps

  1. Put lots of salt in the pot of water
  2. Boil the pot of water
  3. Put the pasta when the water boil
  4. Undercook the pasta
  5. Cook the minced garlic and chili in the pan
  6. Put the pasta with some pasta water in
  7. Serve and enjoy!
